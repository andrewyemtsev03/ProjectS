From Dedication to Recognition - We Are a Top Employer 2026 💙

STADA has once again been recognized as a Top Employer Europe 2026 by the Top Employers Institute - for the fifth consecutive year. In addition, Germany received the certification for the sixth time, alongside Spain, France, the UK, Serbia, and Kazakhstan, which were also awarded national Top Employer certifications.

This recognition is more than a certificate. It reflects the culture we live every day and how we continue to strengthen our commitment to our people - by empowering unique talents to grow and lead with confidence, encouraging ownership and agility in a fast-changing world, and fostering a culture of well-being and uniqueness where every voice is valued.

“Being recognized as a Top Employer 2026 reflects what truly matters at STADA: our people,” says Simone Berger, Chief People Officer. “Their passion, collaboration, and commitment to our purpose create workplaces where everyone can grow and truly belong.”

This achievement once again shows that STADA’s strength lies in its DNA - our purpose, our values, and our shared commitment to caring for people’s health. Today, we celebrate a milestone that reflects exactly that.
